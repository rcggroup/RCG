About us

Vision

The vision of RCG is the realization of modern, effective, innovative, accountable and professional Strategic Public Governance in the Middle East and Africa and to be a key player in setting the Strategic Public Governance Agenda Worldwide..

Mission

Support governments, Agencies, International Organizations & Big corporations in their efforts to maximize return on investment, Public-policy outcomes, Improving the quality of life & Economic well-being of people.

Profile

Public and private organizations in the Middle East and Africa are outgoing constant Organizational change in order to deal with demands for more and better services, lower costs, higher quality, and greater efficiency.
